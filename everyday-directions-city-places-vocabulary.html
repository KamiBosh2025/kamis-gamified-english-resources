Everyday Directions & City Places Vocabulary

Grades 6–9 · A1–A2 · Vocabulary · Directions · City Places

A bilingual 38-question quiz for older students that practises city places, prepositions of place, and common expressions for giving and following directions. Visual clues and Bulgarian support help learners describe locations and navigate a city with confidence.

Learning Goals

  • Recognise and use common vocabulary for places in a city.
  • Understand and apply common prepositions of place.
  • Give and follow simple directions in English.
  • Describe locations using visual clues and bilingual support.
