Kahoot

Playful English & Bulgarian Quiz with GIFs and Emojis

Grades 2–5 · Vocabulary · Listening · Translation · Mixed Skills

A playful 21-question bilingual Kahoot combining funny GIFs, English audio, humorous images and a wide variety of interactive question types. Designed for Grades 2–5, it practises vocabulary, listening, translation, sentence building and everyday English through entertaining tasks that keep young learners actively involved.

Learning Goals

  • Practise everyday English vocabulary in context.
  • Develop listening comprehension through English audio.
  • Translate simple words and expressions between English and Bulgarian.
  • Build sentences and practise spelling through interactive tasks.
  • Respond to visual, humorous and real-life prompts with confidence.
