The Big Bad Wolf Story – Teaching Demonstration

In this detailed teaching demonstration, I play and explain all 26 quiz questions from The Big Bad Wolf Story Kahoot. I present the handwritten-style materials created with INKredible, the AI-generated illustrations designed from my own prompts, and the reasoning behind each of the four answer options. The video also demonstrates Play Solo and Classic modes, scoring and leaderboard features, students’ responses, and the resource’s value for critical thinking, emotional learning and social learning across different age groups.
