Wordwall

Bulgarian–English Fun Word Mix

Grades 5–7 · Vocabulary · Translation · Reading · Listening · Humour

A playful 10-question bilingual Wordwall quiz combining English vocabulary, translation, reading and listening with humorous Bulgarian phrases and colourful images. Learners match meanings, recognise useful expressions and practise understanding English through memorable jokes and wordplay.

Learning Goals

  • Develop English vocabulary through bilingual wordplay.
  • Practise translating short phrases between Bulgarian and English.
  • Improve reading comprehension through humorous questions and answers.
  • Strengthen listening skills through audio-supported tasks.
  • Recognise useful everyday words and expressions in context.
