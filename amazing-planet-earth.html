Amazing Planet Earth

Grades 5–7 · Geography · Nature · General Knowledge · Reading · Listening

A 10-question multimedia Wordwall gameshow combining English with geography, nature and general knowledge. Carefully selected images support comprehension, while alternating professional male and female English voices develop listening skills and expose learners to different voices and pronunciation. Students read, listen and identify fascinating facts about the Earth, including climate records, mountains, deserts, oceans, rivers and countries.

Learning Goals

  • Build general knowledge about the Earth through English.
  • Develop reading comprehension through short factual questions.
  • Improve listening skills with professional male and female English voices.
  • Recognise key facts about climate, mountains, deserts, oceans, rivers and countries.
  • Use visual clues to support understanding and memory.
