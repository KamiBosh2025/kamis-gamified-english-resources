How to Use the Resources

Visitors in Kahoot can open the resource and use Learn, Flashcards or Play solo for individual practice. Host live and Assign are teacher-led options that require a Kahoot account. Play solo offers Classic, Treasure Trove, Tallest Tower and Chill Art. Classic, Treasure Trove and Tallest Tower keep the questions in their original order, while Chill Art shuffles them. Classic is recommended because it keeps the story in sequence and allows learners to focus on the questions without being distracted by collecting gems, building towers, escaping from monsters or completing a jigsaw puzzle.

For classroom use, the teacher can guide the activity on an interactive board. For individual practice, learners can open the resource and work through the questions at their own pace.

Images and text may appear small in the overview, but they can be opened for a clearer view during the activity.

Visitors in Wordwall start the activity directly in the template selected by the resource creator. After completing it, they can choose to try the same content in other available templates — Wordwall offers around 20 interactive and printable template types in total. They can also open the Leaderboard and enter their name using an English keyboard, since Bulgarian input is not yet supported correctly there. The leaderboard shows the participants’ scores, correct answers and completion times. Visitors can also explore other public resources and activities in the Wordwall Community.

Kahoot and Wordwall resources open in a new browser tab. To return to this website, visitors should close the Kahoot or Wordwall tab. The Home buttons on the external platforms do not lead back to this website and may require visitors to sign in.

In addition to interactive activities, both platforms may also offer printable versions of the resources when printing is supported for the selected activity.

Visitor and Account Access

Visitor without an account

Can browse public resources, open Kahoot and Wordwall activities, play them on the external platforms, view teaching demonstrations and explore the public libraries. Visitors without an account cannot save resources to Favourites or download uploaded files. These features are available only to registered users.

Registered user

Has the same public access and can also save resources to a personal Favorites list. Download buttons are available only for files that the author has uploaded and explicitly provided for download.

Administrator

Can access the Admin Panel and manage website resources. The Admin Panel is not visible to visitors or ordinary registered users.

Kahoot and Wordwall activities remain on their original platforms. Saving one of them as a favorite stores a convenient link; it does not create a downloadable copy. Progress can be recorded reliably only for activities completed inside this website or through a future supported platform integration.
