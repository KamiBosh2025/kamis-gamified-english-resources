Wordwall

Click only Present Simple tense!

Grades 5–8 · Grammar · Present Simple · Present Continuous · Reading Speed · Accuracy · Game-Based Learning

A fast-paced 10-level Wordwall game set in a Wild West desert. Learners must quickly hit only the Present Simple sentences while avoiding Present Continuous forms. As the levels increase, more moles appear at once and the sentences disappear faster, demanding rapid reading, concentration and accurate grammatical decisions. Correct answers earn stars, missed sentences receive no points, and incorrect choices are marked with a red cross.

Learning Goals

  • Distinguish Present Simple from Present Continuous quickly and accurately.
  • Recognise affirmative, negative and interrogative sentence forms.
  • Practise forms with do, does, don’t, doesn’t and third-person singular -s.
  • Improve rapid reading, concentration and reaction speed.
  • Build grammatical automaticity through repeated timed practice.
