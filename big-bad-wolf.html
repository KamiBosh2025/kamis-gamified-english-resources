The Big Bad Wolf’s Story

A1–B1 · Reading Comprehension · Mixed Skills · Social-Emotional Learning

A 26-question story quiz in English for guided or individual practice, used successfully with students up to 11th grade.

The activity develops reading comprehension, vocabulary and social-emotional learning through the message that kindness and positive treatment can help even a negative character change.

Learning Goals

  • Understand a longer narrative in English.
  • Follow the sequence of events in a story.
  • Practise vocabulary through context.
  • Discuss kindness, empathy and personal change.
