English & Bulgarian Basics: Grammar, Vocabulary, and Fun Facts

A1–A2 · Grammar · Vocabulary · Translation · Mixed Skills

Practice simple English and Bulgarian: translate words and phrases, spot grammar mistakes, identify the Bulgarian flag, unscramble a sentence, and choose the correct vocabulary. Great for beginners reviewing everyday language and quick true/false facts.

Learning Goals

  • Practise basic English grammar and vocabulary.
  • Translate common words and phrases between English and Bulgarian.
  • Identify and correct simple language mistakes.
  • Develop reading, spelling and sentence-building skills through varied interactive tasks.
