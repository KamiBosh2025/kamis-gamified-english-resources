The Big Bad Wolf’s Story

A1–B1 · Reading Comprehension · Mixed Skills · Social-Emotional Learning

A 26-question story quiz in English for guided or individual practice, used successfully with students up to 11th grade. It develops reading comprehension, vocabulary and social-emotional learning through the message that kindness and positive treatment can help even a negative character change.
