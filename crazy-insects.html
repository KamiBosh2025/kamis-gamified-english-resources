Crazy Insects

Grades 4–6 · English · Biology · Animals · Reading · Listening · General Knowledge

A 12-question multimedia Wordwall quiz combining English with biology, animals and general knowledge. Learners identify insects, compare facts, recognise scientific vocabulary and answer both multiple-choice and true-or-false questions. Carefully selected images and audio support reading and listening, while terms such as metamorphosis, chrysalis and migration expand subject-specific vocabulary.

Learning Goals

  • Build English vocabulary related to insects and animal life.
  • Develop reading comprehension through short factual questions.
  • Improve listening skills with audio-supported answers.
  • Recognise key scientific terms such as metamorphosis and chrysalis.
  • Expand general knowledge through English and biology.
