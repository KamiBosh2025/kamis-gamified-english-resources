Wordwall

Have fun, kids!

Grades 2–6 · Vocabulary · Translation · Word Order · Reading · Game-Based Learning

A fast-paced Wordwall maze game in which learners escape from monsters by entering the hiding place with the correct answer. The activity combines vocabulary, translation, word order and reading practice, while the timer adds excitement and challenge. Younger learners can play with teacher support, while older pupils can complete the maze independently.

Learning Goals

  • Practise English vocabulary through fast-paced gameplay.
  • Match Bulgarian words and phrases with their English equivalents.
  • Improve reading comprehension and word-order recognition.
  • Make quick decisions under time pressure.
  • Develop confidence through repeated game-based practice.
