Bulgaria’s 100 Tourist Sites Challenge

Grades 6–12 · Culture · Geography · History · Tourism · Interdisciplinary Learning

A 33-question bilingual Kahoot for students in Grades 6–12, combining English learning with Bulgarian geography, history, culture and tourism. Learners explore natural landmarks, historic sites, monasteries, fortresses, caves and the traditions of the 100 National Tourist Sites movement. The resource develops cultural awareness, travel knowledge, social skills and interest in Bulgaria. It can also serve as a starting point for STEM and STEAM practical activities, including route planning, digital maps, travel budgeting, distance and time calculations, data collection, presentations and digital tourist guides.

Learning Goals

  • Explore important natural, historical and cultural landmarks in Bulgaria.
  • Connect English learning with geography, history, culture and tourism.
  • Develop cultural awareness, travel knowledge and social skills.
  • Use bilingual clues to discuss places, travel and national traditions.
  • Prepare projects such as travel routes, maps, presentations and digital tourist guides.
  • Use the resource as a starting point for STEM and STEAM practical activities involving planning, calculations, research and digital creation.
